Ashwagandha

Adaptogen

Withania somnifera

Key Phytochemicals: Withanolides (Withaferin A, Withanolide D)
Modern Mechanism of Action:

Modulates HPA (Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Adrenal) axis activity; downregulates serum cortisol levels to reduce systemic biochemical stress.

Source Ref: Charaka Samhita, Chikitsa Sthana

Haridra (Turmeric)

Anti-Inflammatory

Curcuma longa

Key Phytochemicals: Curcuminoids (Curcumin, Demethoxycurcumin)
Modern Mechanism of Action:

Inhibits NF-kB transcriptive pathway, effectively downregulating inflammatory cytokines (TNF-alpha and IL-6) and COX-2 enzymes.

Source Ref: Sushruta Samhita, Sutra Sthana

Tulsi

Immunomodulator

Ocimum sanctum

Key Phytochemicals: Eugenol, Carvacrol, Ursolic Acid
Modern Mechanism of Action:

Enhances NK (Natural Killer) cell and T-helper cell activity; high phenolic composition scavengerizes free radicals protecting cellular structures.

Source Ref: Dhavantari Nighantu
